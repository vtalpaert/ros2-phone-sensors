CDC Serial — boards with native USB

Use this for boards that connect directly over USB without a separate converter chip: Teensy (any model), Arduino Leonardo, Arduino Micro, Arduino Zero, Arduino MKR series, Arduino Nano 33 IoT. These boards implement the USB CDC-ACM standard, which allows direct serial communication over USB.

CP2102 — USB to UART converter

Use this for USB-to-UART converter dongles based on the Silicon Labs CP2102 chip (vendorId 0x10c4, productId 0xea60). These converters are also found on many ESP32 development boards. Connect the converter's TX/RX pins to your microcontroller's RX/TX pins to bridge serial communication over USB. The baud rate must match the one configured on your microcontroller.
